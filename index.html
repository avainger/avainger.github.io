First Coding Experience

Posted on November 2019

I never thought I would enjoy coding. My partner is an IT consultant and one of the things he does on his sparetime is coding. I have always been curious about what he does, I wanted to be a part of his world. I was never good at IT even during my secondary school. So it was not until lately that I really took interest in coding. Partly because I wanted to understand my partner's world and talk about it, but also I realised that web developing and other IT jobs are the most in demand careers these days. So I decided to get out of my comfort zone and learn coding. I have to say, I have only learned a few so far but I am enjoying every second of it. I hope I can be successful in this.
